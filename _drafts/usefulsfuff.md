## Fairlearn
Fairlearn is an open-source, community-driven project and Python library designed to help data scientists and developers assess and improve the fairness of artificial intelligence systems. The toolkit focuses on identifying and mitigating "harms" to specific groups of people—such as those defined by race, gender, or age—by providing specialized metrics for evaluation and algorithms for bias mitigation. Beyond its technical tools, the project emphasizes that fairness is a "sociotechnical" challenge, offering educational resources and case studies to help practitioners navigate the complex trade-offs between model performance and equity.
