# About Me

The intersection of artificial intelligence and enterprise risk is where the future of tech regulation is being written. 

With active **Certified Regulatory Compliance Manager (CRCM)** and **Certified Enterprise Risk Professional (CERP)** credentials, I bring a rigorous, audit-ready perspective to technical environments. My work focuses on translating abstract governance frameworks into executable Python code, data pipelines, and LLM implementations.

### My Approach to AI Governance
* **For the Business:** Lowering compliance costs and accelerating review times through intelligent automation (e.g., using LLMs to process massive datasets like CFPB complaints).
* **For the Engineers:** Embedding risk, bias mitigation, and data privacy controls directly into the development lifecycle, ensuring models are safe before deployment.
* **For the Regulators:** Building systems with transparent, explainable, and reproducible architectures.

### Certifications
* **CRCM** (Certified Regulatory Compliance Manager) — *Active since 2022*
* **CERP** (Certified Enterprise Risk Professional) — *Active since 2023*
