In August 2022, I successfully completed the CRCM examination. Below is a summary of my preparation efforts with links to resources I used and created. Hopefully, this information will be helpful to anyone currently planning to take the exam. 

## ABA's CRCM Exam Online Prep Course
My primary study resource for the exam was the [ABA's CRCM Exam Online Prep course](https://www.aba.com/training-events/online-training/crcm-exam-online-prep). The couse features a comprehensive series of videos by [Carl Pry](https://www.linkedin.com/in/carlpry/), which I found exceptionally well-organized and easy to digest. 
- Pros: Includes a full practice exam and high-quality video lectures.
- Cons: The price point is significant—$1,045 for non-members (as of 2026).
If your budget (or your employer's) allows for it, this is an excellent resource for preparation.

## Study Notes
While moving through the modules, I synthesized the material into my own study notes, supplementing them with external sources. I found that the act of **writing** the notes was just as important as reading them later. You can view a copy of my [CRCM study notes here](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1Tc6tIyqIMUxUTMJjxdTLKdImaeYK0NDG2vA4HnF4Gh0/edit?usp=sharing).

## Flash Cards
I also created a big stack of flash cards to remember key terms and rules. This was a very effective study method, allowing me to drill myself until I had everything memorized. 

## The Exam
I sat for the exam at a proctored testing center. Be prepared for strict security; you'll need to store all personal items (keys, phone, wallet) in a locker before entering. One of the best features of the computer-based test is that the screen will notify you of your pass/fail status immediately upon completion.
