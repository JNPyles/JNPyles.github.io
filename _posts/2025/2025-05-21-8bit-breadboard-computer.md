I am building an 8-bit breadboard computer together with my son as a fun way to learn how computers work. We are using Ben Eater's [8-bit Breadboard kit](https://eater.net/8bit/kits) and following along with his [Youtube course](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLowKtXNTBypGqImE405J2565dvjafglHU).

The Arithmetic Logic Unit (ALU) is complete and can add and subtract 8-bit numbers: 

![ALU_GIF](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/77cb6284-b2e1-497d-b318-f1efe95fa001)

We are taking a break from the project while my son is in school so he can focus on his studies, but I hope to complete the project this summer (2026).
