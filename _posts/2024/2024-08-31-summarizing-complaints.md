In the LinkedIn article linked above, I share what I learned using Large Language Models (LLMs) like ChatGPT and Llama to quickly summarize a large number of complaint narratives. Key findings: 

- Using natural language models to generate summaries of complaints can significantly enhance the complaint management, allowing analysts and management to quickly review complaints. 
- These summaries can omit important details, so human review is still needed.
